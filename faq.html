Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start trading?

Start with education: read the modules, open a demo account, practice journaling, and learn to manage risk. Then scale slowly.

What is the minimum I need?

Depends on instrument. Stocks: fractional shares let you start small. Options: more capital is helpful for diversification. Never trade more than you can afford to lose.

Which platforms do you recommend?

TradingView for charts. thinkorswim, Interactive Brokers, Webull for options. Choose regulated brokers with good fills and options tools.
