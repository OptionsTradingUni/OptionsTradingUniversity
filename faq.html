Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start trading?

Learn basics (start with Guides), open a broker demo account, paper trade, then start small. Journal every trade.

What’s the minimum to start?

Stocks: fractional shares let you start low (<$100). Options: depends on strategy, start small. Forex: micro accounts from $5–$50.

Which platforms do you recommend?

TradingView for charts, thinkorswim/Webull for options, Robinhood for simple orders. Choose regulated brokers with good options tools.

Do you guarantee profits?

No. We teach strategies and discipline — trading carries risk. Protect capital and follow risk rules.
