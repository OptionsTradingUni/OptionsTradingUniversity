Get in Touch

How do I contact OTU? +

You can reach us anytime by email at support@optionstradinguni.com or by direct message on TikTok. We typically respond within 24 hours.

How do I join the mentorship group? +

Membership is by invitation only. Send us a DM on TikTok with the suggested message, and one of our mentors will guide you through the steps. The current membership fee is $50.

What’s the best way to get support? +

For technical issues with the site, email us directly. For trading-related questions, message a mentor on TikTok. Once inside the community, you’ll also gain access to our support chatroom.

When should I expect a reply? +

Our support team replies within 24 hours during weekdays. Mentors may respond faster, especially during active trading hours. Weekend replies may take a little longer.
