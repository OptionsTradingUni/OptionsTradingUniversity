Comprehensive Glossary

CALL — Option to buy underlying at strike.
PUT — Option to sell underlying at strike.
STRIKE PRICE — Price controlled by the option.
PREMIUM — Cost of the option contract.
EXPIRATION — Date the option ceases to exist.
IN THE MONEY (ITM) — Option has intrinsic value.
OUT OF THE MONEY (OTM) — No intrinsic value.
AT THE MONEY (ATM) — Strike near current price.
DELTA — Option sensitivity to price move.
THETA — Time decay of option value.
GAMMA — Rate of change of Delta.
VEGA — Sensitivity to implied volatility.
IMPLIED VOLATILITY (IV) — Market's expected future volatility.
OPEN INTEREST — Number of open contracts.
VOLUME — Contracts traded on a day.
BID / ASK — Buyers' bid vs sellers' ask price.
SWEEP — Aggressive buy across exchanges.
BLOCK — Large order placed discreetly.
LOTTO — Cheap short-dated high-risk option play.
SCALP — Very short term trade for small gains.
BTFD — "Buy the f* dip".
WICKED OUT — Stopped out by a wick spike.
ROLL — Move option to different strike/expiry.
ATH / ATL — All-time high / low.
RISK/REWARD — Ratio between possible loss and potential gain.
LEVERAGE — Using borrowed capital or options to increase exposure.
